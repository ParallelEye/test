About

The construction of artificial scene

Transform the real urban road information into the corresponding virtual city road information. Then add 3D models such as houses, roads, trees, lane lines to virtual city through rapid modeling.

Simulated environmental conditions

In order to increase the diversity and fidelity of artificial scene, we control the parameters in the script, such as the material, the shader, and the simulated environmental conditions.

Generation of Ground-Truth Annotations

Ground-truth annotations are essential for the design and evaluation of vision algorithm. Unity3D is used to automatically generate accurate ground-truth labels: depth, optical flow, object tracking, object detection, instance segmentation, and semantic segmentation.

Properties of ParallelEye

The ParallelEye dataset consists of 21777 frames of the city taken from a virtual car moving randomly through the city. ParallelEye has four sub datasets

Parallel vision is an extension of the ACP approach(Artificial societies, Computationalexperiments, and Parallel execution) into the computer vision field.

For parallel vision, photo-realistic artificial scenes are used to model and represent complex real scenes, computational experiments are utilized to learn and evaluate a variety of vision models, and parallel execution is conducted to optimize the vision system online and realize perception and understanding of complex scenes.
